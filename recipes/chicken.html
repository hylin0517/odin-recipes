Ingredients

  • 200g Chicken Breast
  • 3 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 & 1/2 tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp Cooking Wine
  • 1/2 tbsp Sugar

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breast into small cube pieces.
  2. Put the cubes into a bowl and add all of the flavoring into the bowl and mix well
  3. Let the chicken marinate for 15 minutes or overnight
  4. Preheat the pan and add oil into the pan
  5. Cook the chicken for about 1 minute on each side or until cripsy brown
